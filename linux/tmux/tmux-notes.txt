<tmux> to enter tmux server

tmux new -s <name of new sessions>

tmux new-session -s <name of new sessions> (updated preferred naming command)


tmux attach -t <name of session>

To detach from the tmux session and keep it running in the background, you can press the keys ctrl + b then d. This will detach from the session and keep it running in the background.

tmux list-sessions

This will list all the sessions including those that are detached.

tmux kill-session -t <name of session>
